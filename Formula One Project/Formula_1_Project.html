F1 Terms!

Some F1 Terms you might want to know

Protective Barrier put in place to prtoect a drivers neck during racing

All refer what postion/place a driver finished a race in
Podium
1st,2nd,3rd place stand
Grand Prix
The name of each race a formula one driver races as per competion
Drivers Champion
Driver who won most races overall in the season
World Championship
